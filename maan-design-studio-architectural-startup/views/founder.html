Mahsa, the founder of MAAN Studio, finished her architecture school from University of Tehran, Iran in 2014 and Master of Project Management from UNSW Australia in 2015. She brings a rich and diverse architectural background honed through years of working with globally recognized firms. Her experience includes serving as a Project Manager at Alloy Development in New York City and as an Architectural Designer at Kohn Pedersen Fox Associates (KPF), as well as collaborating to high-profile projects across a wide range of sectors including residential, commercials, and mixed-use developments. In 2024, Mahsa realized her long-life ambition by establishing MAAN Studio. Her international experience, combined with her unique background in New York City - working in both architectural and real estate development firms - has equipped her with a distinctive design philosophy - one that merges global best practices with local sensibilities, creating bespoke, innovative designs tailored to each project's context and budget.
